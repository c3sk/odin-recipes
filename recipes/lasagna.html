Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable fillings, and creamy sauces like béchamel. Traditionally, it includes a meat ragu, béchamel sauce, and grated cheese, all baked to a golden, bubbly perfection.

Versatile and customizable, lasagna can be adapted with various ingredients such as roasted vegetables or seafood. It is typically served hot, offering a comforting and hearty meal, often paired with a simple green salad and a glass of red wine.
